Mission Statement

Our mission is to help the residents of Longwood Apartments in Columbia by ensuring their food security and to bring attention to the issues that surround food security. We aim to operate on zero overhead and are ran entirely by student interns taking ASIA 399 at the University of Maryland, Baltimore Country.

What we do

The Asian Studies Programs food pantry started in the Spring of 2014 with assistance from a Breaking Ground Grant from the University of Maryland, Baltimore Country. We provide food to assist low- income Asian senior citizens residing at Longwood Apartments in Columbia, Maryland in order to ensure their food security throughout the month. Interns enrolled in ASIA 399 at UMBC run the program and assist in translating on monthly donation days in addition to many other tasks. The program operates on donations from red donations boxes set up around the UMBC campus community. Other organizations involved in the project in the area include Asian and non-Asian churches, Boy Scouts, two Chinese schools in Howard county, and several local businesses. In the future we hope to function on zero overhead costs.

In the first semester of the program, 20 residents requested food. By Fall of 2014, that number has risen to 90 residents a 350% increase!

Frequently Asked Questions


Where do I bring food collections to?
You can bring them to the red boxes on UMBC’s campus or you can contact Julie Rosenthal to set up a time to drop by your collections or our interns can also come pick them up from you.

What kind of food are you looking for?
We are looking for non-perishable food donations. Primarily Asian noodles, seaweed, canned tuna, spam, canned mackerel, peanuts, soybeans, canned fruit, and canned vegetables.

What do I have to do to become an intern?
Interns are UMBC students. Anyone who attends UMBC can become one if they are qualified and pass interview selections. We are looking for interns who can translate and interns who can help with the coordination of the food pantry. 40 hours of work count towards each credit hour.

How often do you deliver donations?
We deliver donations on a monthly basis.
